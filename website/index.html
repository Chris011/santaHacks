Secret Santa is a Western Christmas tradition in which members of a group or community are randomly assigned a person to whom they anonymously give a gift. START YOUR OWN SECRET SANTA

How does it work?

1. Fill Out The Form For Your Organization

Click the "Start Your Own Secret Santa" button. Then fill out the following form for your group!

2. Users Submit Information

Santa Hacks will generate a webpage that is unique for each team. Team members will enter in their information by using the customized page, and be paired up by Santa Hacks.

3. Inform Users

After being matched, we give each participant an email with his peer name and an optional admin message.

4. Buy a Gift

Everything is now setup. Just go buy a gift for your peer before your party. Merry Christmas!

Email sent to users

Each user will receive an email to inform them of their peer name. You can provide an optional message to inform your team about budget or deadline.

Why do I need this?

Some organizations do a Secret Santa every year. This site makes it easier to connect the community by letting organizations personalize the questions based on the communities interests.

For example, if our computer science group wished to do a secret santa, we can have the form ask what their favorite OS is, their favorite programming language, etc. This allows a group to get to know each other better without resorting to vague questions such as 'what is your favorite color'
